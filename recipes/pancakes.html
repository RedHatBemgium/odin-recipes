Pancakes

The Best Homemade Pancakes

This easy pancake recipe makes light and fluffy pancakes from scratch. Perfect for breakfast or brunch, they are quick to whip up and taste amazing.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients:

    In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.

  2. Whisk the wet ingredients:

    In a separate bowl, beat the egg and then add the milk and melted butter. Whisk until well combined.

  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients:

    Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and stir gently until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; the batter should still be slightly lumpy.

  4. Preheat a griddle or pan:

    Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.

  5. Cook the pancakes:

    Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using about 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

  6. Serve:

    Serve the pancakes warm with your choice of toppings such as butter, syrup, fresh berries, or whipped cream.
